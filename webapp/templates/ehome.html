The Future Is Here.


Have you ever imagined how you would survive a secret, high stakes mission? You can find out by living it. Laser Maze is an Action-Packed Role Playing game where players have to infiltrate a high-security area in order to succeed in their mission in a given amount of time.


You will have to complete a series of skill based tasks while avoiding laser beams in order to make progress. Cutting the laser beam adds a time penalty.


The game is designed to push your strategy skills to their limit, and test your physical and mental prowess.
