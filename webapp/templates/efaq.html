▲ ▼ Can I play Laser Maze with no prior endurance training/experience?
Yes, Laser Maze is a fun and safe role playing game that does not require any prior training.
▲ ▼ Can I play alone?
Yes, this game can be played by a single player or a group.
▲ ▼ How long does each game span for?
Laser Maze has a time limit of 30 minutes. The goal is to finish as soon as possible!
▲ ▼ How many people can play at once?
Up to 6 people can play at once.
▲ ▼ What are the timings?
Laser Maze is open between 11 am and 11 pm, 7 days a week.
▲ ▼ Can we come in and play whenever we like?
All players must book slots atleast 5 hours beforehand, slots subject to availability. If you're an Agon Member, you can book upto 1 hour before you come.
▲ ▼ I would like to bring my company team over for a day out.
We love having big groups over and also organise additional activities for these settings. You can contact us for more details.
▲ ▼ I would like to host a Laser Maze Space. How do I learn more?
We love bringing enthusiastic franchisees on board. You can explore our website to learn about the game and why hosting a space would be beneficial to you, and contact us anytime for more.
